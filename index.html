Simulator of shooting a pistols Beretta M9, Luger P08, Colt 1911, Makarov and Desert Eagle. The main feature of the «Pistol shooting» game that distinguish it from other similar games is a choice of aiming mode with «alignment of a front sight». What is «alignment of a front sight»? In most simulations of fire, when aiming, you just have to combine the sight of the guns weapon with the target and shoot. This pistol sight consists of two parts, front sight and rear sight with a cut. To shoot accurately it is necessary to put the front sight on the center of the cut and its top end is aligned to the top plane of rear sight. This is called alignment of the front sight. Keeping this mutual location of sighting devices it is necessary to combine the guns sight with the target and shoot.
